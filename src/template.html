About me

Having entered the world of programming, I became intensely interested in backend development. I am a coding buff who feels as though every instace of typing codes and sovling problems are highlights of one's life. I am a forever-student who enjoys the sheer act of learning something. I believe that the true happiness comes from mindfully delving into each moment of doing whatever you are doing at the moment. My ultimate goal is to live a good life and it consists of treating each day as a new opportunity to hone my skills to focus on the present.

After having wandered here and there, from film/tv production to working at a bar, I have seen many kinds of people and learned general idea of what life can offer. I started believing that life does not automatically offer some meaning to me directly and that I must start finding my own meaning rather than expecting something to define my life, which cannot be simply defined. In conclusion, my mind was ready to leap into an unknown adventure. Then I was told that a thinker like myself would enjoy studying Computer Science. Eventually, I decided to go get a Master's Degree in Computer Science after learning how to code. That's when I signed up for Wecode Intensive Coding Bootcamp. This was an indispensible experience during which I learned that programming is my calling. Problem-solving with other programmers also taught me that if I do this for a living, it would be a dream-come-true. And thus the next stage of my life as a backend-developer started.
